FLOWER DELIVERY BY GARDENIA, THE FLORIST WITH A FRENCH TOUCH

Where do our florists deliver? Our florists provide same day flower delivery in NYC, same day flower delivery in Chicago and next day flower delivery to over 1,400 cities in the Northeast. Whether your flowers are being delivered to a residence, office, hospital, school, or hotel, our florists are here to help them arrive safely and on time. Same Day Flower Delivery in NYC Our staff delivers to all Manhattan neighborhoods (including the Upper East Side, Times Square, the West Village, Soho, Chelsea, Tribeca and many more!) and most Brooklyn areas (including Williamsburg, Brooklyn Heights, Dumbo, Vinegar Hill, Bushwick, Boerum Hill, and Carroll Gardens) with the choice of three delivery windows (9:00am to 1:00pm, 1:00pm to 5:00pm and 5:00pm to 7:00pm on weekdays). Our cut-off time for same day delivery is 5:00pm that day. Our florists even deliver on Sundays with an order cut-off time of Saturday at 12:00pm. Same Day Flower Delivery in Chicago From our central location in West Town, Chicago, we offer same day flower delivery to many area neighborhoods, from North Side to South Side and everything in between. We deliver to River North, Old Town, the Loop, South Loop, West Loop, West Town, Wicker Park, Lincoln Park & Lakeview, Logan Square, Uptown, Wrigleyville, Gold Coast and many more. You can also have them hand-delivered to a wide variety of Chicago businesses, hospitals, hotels and nursing homes. Send the perfect message to someone you care about — a beautiful bouquet of flowers. Next Day Flower Delivery to most cities in the Northeast For loved ones living outside of Manhattan, Ode à la Rose also offers next day delivery to most cities in the Northeast. We deliver to other NYC boroughs (including Queens, Staten Island and the Bronx), New Jersey, Pennsylvania, Virginia, Delaware, Maryland, Rhode Island, Massachusetts, Connecticut and New Hampshire. All our bouquets are made fresh the day before and sent overnight to ensure the flowers are received looking just as they did when they left our workshop. Next day delivery is available from Monday through Friday only and orders must be placed by 5:00pm the day before. For Monday deliveries, the cut-off time for orders is Saturday at 12:00pm
